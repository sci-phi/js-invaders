Js-Invaders

A minimal implementation of the classic 'Space Invaders' game

Download as .zip Download as .tar.gz View on GitHub

Can you survive the alien invasion?


How To Play

  • Fire shots at the invaders with the space bar
  • Avoid invader shots with the left and right arrow keys
  • Clear all invaders from the level to survive
